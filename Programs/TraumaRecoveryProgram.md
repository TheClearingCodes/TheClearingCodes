# Program: Trauma Recovery

A structured multi-session clearing sequence for trauma — acute events, complex/developmental trauma, PTSD, and the lasting effects of overwhelming experiences.

> **Important:** This program is an energetic clearing practice. It is not a substitute for professional trauma therapy. If you are experiencing active PTSD symptoms, flashbacks, dissociation, or suicidal thoughts, please seek professional support. This program works alongside — not in place of — qualified care. If at any point during these sessions you feel overwhelmed, pause, ground yourself, and return when you feel ready.

---

## Why a Targeted Program?

Trauma lodges in the body, the nervous system, the subconscious, and the energy field simultaneously. It creates survival patterns that persist long after the danger has passed. Energetic clearing addresses the dimensions of trauma that talk therapy alone may not reach — the energetic imprints, cellular memories, ancestral patterns, and field distortions that keep the system locked in survival mode.

## Important: The Pace of Trauma Clearing

The Protocol's Graceful Clearing Rule ensures that clearings occur at the pace your system can handle. However, with trauma specifically:

- **Go slowly.** Allow 3-7 days between sessions — more if needed.
- **Trust your body.** If it says slow down, slow down.
- **You do not need to re-experience the trauma to clear it.** The clearing process works energetically.
- **Ground between sessions.** Walk in nature, take baths, sleep extra, eat well.
- **Seek support.** Consider working with a therapist alongside this program.

## After Completing the Program

- Healing from trauma is ongoing — be patient and compassionate with yourself
- Continue professional support alongside energetic clearing
- Run daily maintenance clearings and clear triggers as they arise
- Build safety: safe people, safe environments, safe practices
- The program can be repeated as deeper layers surface
- Celebrate your courage — doing this work takes immense bravery
