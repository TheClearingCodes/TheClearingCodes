# Program: Relationship Healing

A structured multi-session clearing sequence for relationship wounds, attachment patterns, codependency, and interpersonal dynamics.

---

> **Important:** This program is an energetic clearing practice. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease, and is not a substitute for professional care.
## Why a Targeted Program?

Relationship patterns run deep. They are shaped by childhood attachment, ancestral templates, past-life connections, cultural programming, and accumulated emotional wounds. A focused program systematically addresses each layer — from current dynamics to the deepest roots — allowing genuine transformation in how you relate to others.

## The 5-Session Sequence

Allow at least 1-3 days between sessions. Relationship clearing often brings up strong emotions — honor whatever arises.

---

### Session 1: Current Relationship Patterns & Cords

**Focus:** Clear the surface-level patterns, unhealthy dynamics, and energetic cords in your current relationships.

**Step 1 — Center**
> Take a breath. Bring your awareness to the present moment.

**Step 2 — Set Intention**
> "I now focus on clearing all unhealthy relationship patterns, dynamics, and energetic cords that are affecting my current relationships — romantic, familial, and interpersonal."

**Step 3 — Retrieve**
> "All of the unhealthy dynamics, codependent patterns, energetic cords, enmeshments, power imbalances, and toxic bonds in my current relationships? REVEAL, RECEIVE, RECALL, RETRIEVE." (x3)

**Step 4 — Clear**
> "I now clear: all codependency, enmeshment, and unhealthy attachment in my relationships. All energetic cords that drain, control, or limit me. All people-pleasing, caretaking, and self-abandonment patterns. All power imbalances — giving my power away or attempting to control others. All communication blocks and patterns of withholding, avoidance, or reactivity. All projections I place on others and projections others have placed on me."
>
> "Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds, Vows & Verdicts, Notions & Nines.
> Patterns & Programs, Breaks & Bytes, Roots & Remnants, Ridges & Rites.
> Release, restore, reclaim, refine; all levels and layers, all space and all time."

**Step 5 — Contracts**
> "I rescind all contracts to sacrifice myself for others' approval. I dissolve all agreements to stay in dynamics that diminish me. I revoke all vows of obligation that override my wellbeing."
>
> "RESCIND, REVOKE, RECANT, RENOUNCE."

**Step 6 — Install**
> "I now download and activate: healthy relationship boundaries, authentic communication, mutual respect, the ability to love without losing myself, and sovereign connection. RECEIVE, INSTALL, INTEGRATE."

**Step 7 — Seal**
> "REPAIR, RESET, ANCHOR, LOCK AND SEAL."
