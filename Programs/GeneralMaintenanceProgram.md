# Program: General Maintenance & Optimization

A structured multi-session clearing sequence for overall energetic maintenance, field optimization, and comprehensive clearing across all life areas. This is the recommended starting program for anyone not working on a specific issue.

---

> **Note:** Unlike the issue-specific programs that go deep on one topic, this program cycles broadly across all major areas of life — providing a thorough energetic "tune-up" from surface to root. It is an excellent first program for beginners and a valuable periodic reset for experienced practitioners.

## Why a General Maintenance Program?

Energy accumulates in layers. Daily life brings stress, other people's projections, environmental frequencies, unprocessed emotions, and inherited patterns — all stacking quietly in your field. Even without a specific "issue," this buildup creates fog, fatigue, reactivity, and a general feeling of being weighed down. A structured general clearing addresses everything systematically, layer by layer, so nothing gets missed.
