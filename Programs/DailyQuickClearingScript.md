# Daily Quick Clearing Scripts

Ready-to-use clearing scripts in three lengths. Pick the version that fits your time and read it aloud (or silently). No preparation needed — just start.

> **Tip:** These same scripts are available interactively at [theclearingcodes.com](https://theclearingcodes.com) via the Clearing Tool, where you can also customize them with specific activations.

## Short Version (~30 seconds)

Use this when you're short on time, between tasks, or just need a quick reset.

**1. Center**
> Take a breath. Be present.

**2. Clear**
> "Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds. Release, restore, reclaim, refine."

**3. Install**
> "I receive the highest frequencies now."

**4. Seal**
> "Anchor, lock and seal."

---

## Standard Version (~2-5 minutes)

A solid daily clearing that covers the essentials. Good for morning or evening use.

**1. Center**
> Take a breath. Bring your awareness to the present moment.

**2. Retrieve**
> "What needs to clear from my field today? REVEAL, RECEIVE, RECALL, RETRIEVE." (x3)

**3. Clear**
> "Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds. Release, restore, reclaim, refine."

**4. Contracts**
> "RESCIND, REVOKE, RECANT, RENOUNCE."

**5. Install**
> "I download the highest Life-Affirming frequencies available to me now. RECEIVE, INSTALL, INTEGRATE."

**6. Seal**
> "REPAIR, RESET, ANCHOR, LOCK AND SEAL."

---

## Full Version (~10-15 minutes)

The complete clearing process with all steps. Recommended at least once per week, or whenever you want a thorough clearing.

**1. Center**
> Take a breath. Bring your full awareness to the present moment. Set the intention that this clearing works for your highest good.

**2. Retrieve**
> "All of the decisions, beliefs, agreements, attachments, distortions, and interference — known and unknown — across all layers, levels, and timelines."
>
> "REVEAL, RECEIVE, RECALL, RETRIEVE." (State 3 times)

**3. Clear**
> "Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds, Vows & Verdicts, Notions & Nines.
> Release, restore, reclaim, refine; all levels and layers, all space and all time."

**4. Contracts**
> "RESCIND, REVOKE, RECANT, RENOUNCE."

**5. Final Sweep**
> "Everything covert, concealed, hidden, or cloaked — and everything anchoring it in place — is cleared, dissolved, and transmuted now."
>
> [Repeat Clearing Command]
> "Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds. Release, restore, reclaim, refine.
> All levels and layers, all space and all time."

**6. Install**
> "I download, activate, and integrate the highest Life-Affirming frequencies available to me now — throughout every layer and dimension of my being, for my highest good and the highest good of all. RECEIVE, INSTALL, INTEGRATE."

**7. Recall**
> "I call back all my essences, energies, and sovereign power — in full alignment with my Original Life-Affirming blueprints — across all levels and layers of my being, now."
>
> "RETURN, RECLAIM, RESTORE, RENEW." (State 3 times)

**8. Seal**
> "I reset, repair, and recalibrate all my fields, systems, and energies to perfect functioning — in alignment with my Original Life-Affirming blueprints. All reversals are corrected now. Anchor, lock and seal this work across all fields and all time."
>
> "REPAIR, RESET, ANCHOR, LOCK AND SEAL." (State 3 times)

## When to Use Each Version

| Version | Best For |
|---------|----------|
| **Short** | Quick resets between tasks, after difficult interactions, midday energy dips |
| **Standard** | Daily morning or evening clearing, maintaining a clean field |
| **Full** | Weekly deep clear, after stressful periods, when you feel heavy or off, first clearing of the day |

## Adding Custom Activations

After any Install step, you can add specific qualities you want to activate. Simply state:

> "I now download and activate: [your chosen qualities]."

**Examples:**
- "I now download and activate: confidence, clarity, and creative flow."
- "I now download and activate: deep rest, peaceful sleep, and overnight integration."
- "I now download and activate: calm focus, productivity, and joyful engagement with my work."
