# Program: Self-Worth & Confidence

A structured multi-session clearing sequence for low self-worth, lack of confidence, self-doubt, shame, and not-enoughness.

---

> **Important:** This program is an energetic clearing practice. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease, and is not a substitute for professional care.
## Why a Targeted Program?

Self-worth issues are among the most pervasive and foundational blocks people carry. They infiltrate every area of life — relationships, career, health, finances, creativity, and spiritual growth. Because worthiness wounds run so deep (often formed before conscious memory), they require systematic, layered clearing.

## The 5-Session Sequence

---

### Session 1: The "Not Enough" Story

**Focus:** Clear the surface-level self-doubt, the inner critic, and the "not enough" narrative.

**Step 1 — Center**
> Take a breath. Bring your awareness to the present moment.

**Step 2 — Set Intention**
> "I now clear all forms of the 'not enough' story — the self-doubt, the inner critic, and the persistent belief that I am less than."

**Step 3 — Retrieve**
> "All of the beliefs, stories, and patterns telling me I am not enough — not good enough, smart enough, attractive enough, successful enough, lovable enough? REVEAL, RECEIVE, RECALL, RETRIEVE." (x3)

**Step 4 — Clear**
> "I now clear: all 'I'm not enough' beliefs in all their forms. All STANCE around my worthiness — every fixed position, posture, and orientation I hold about my value. All SCRIPT — every rehearsed narrative, inner monologue, and self-defining story telling me I am less than. All inner critic voices and self-deprecating thoughts. All comparison to others that diminishes me. All perfectionism — the belief that I must be flawless to be worthy. All self-doubt and second-guessing. All apologizing for existing and shrinking to make others comfortable. All imposter syndrome in every area of my life. All beliefs that I need to earn, prove, or justify my worth."
>
> "Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds, Vows & Verdicts, Notions & Nines.
> Patterns & Programs, Breaks & Bytes, Roots & Remnants, Ridges & Rites.
> Release, restore, reclaim, refine; all levels and layers, all space and all time."

**Step 5 — Contracts**
> "I rescind all agreements to believe I am not enough. I dissolve all contracts with self-doubt, self-criticism, and unworthiness."
>
> "RESCIND, REVOKE, RECANT, RENOUNCE."

**Step 6 — Install**
> "I now download and activate: self-acceptance, self-respect, quiet confidence, and the knowing that I am enough exactly as I am. RECEIVE, INSTALL, INTEGRATE."

**Step 7 — Seal**
> "REPAIR, RESET, ANCHOR, LOCK AND SEAL."

## After Completing the Program

- Practice receiving compliments, help, and good things without deflecting
- Notice when the old unworthiness pattern tries to return — clear it immediately
- Speak to yourself as you would speak to someone you love
- Celebrate your wins, no matter how small
- The program can be repeated as deeper layers surface
