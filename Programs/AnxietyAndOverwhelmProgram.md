# Program: Anxiety & Overwhelm

A structured multi-session clearing sequence for chronic anxiety, panic, nervous system dysregulation, and overwhelm.

---

> **Important:** This program is an energetic clearing practice. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease, and is not a substitute for professional care.
## Why a Targeted Program?

Anxiety rarely has a single cause. It weaves through mental patterns, emotional wounds, nervous system programming, energetic sensitivity, ancestral conditioning, and subconscious survival mechanisms. A general clearing addresses all of these, but a focused program goes deeper — systematically clearing each root layer across multiple sessions so the system can integrate before the next layer is addressed.

## The 5-Session Sequence

Allow at least 1-3 days between sessions for integration. Listen to your body — if a session brings up a lot, wait longer before proceeding.

All nervous system dysregulation and hypervigilance. All SOMA — all body-held anxiety, somatic tension, and the physical imprint of fear stored in my muscles, gut, chest, and nervous system. > Release, restore, reclaim, refine; all levels and layers, all space and all time."

> "I rescind, revoke, renounce, and dissolve all agreements to live in fear, to stay hypervigilant, and to believe I am not safe."

> "I now download and activate: calm, safety, peace, trust, groundedness, and nervous system regulation. My body remembers what safety feels like. RECEIVE, INSTALL, INTEGRATE."

> "REPAIR, RESET, ANCHOR, LOCK AND SEAL."
