# Program: Financial Abundance

A structured multi-session clearing sequence for financial blocks, money wounds, scarcity programming, and abundance resistance.

---

> **Important:** This program is an energetic clearing practice. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease, and is not a substitute for professional care.
## Why a Targeted Program?

Financial patterns are among the most deeply layered issues people carry. They involve conscious beliefs, subconscious programming, ancestral poverty patterns, cultural conditioning, religious vows, and energetic structures that actively repel abundance. This program systematically addresses each layer so your relationship with money can transform at the root level.
