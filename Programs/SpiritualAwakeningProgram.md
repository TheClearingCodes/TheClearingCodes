# Program: Spiritual Awakening & Expansion

A structured multi-session clearing sequence for those navigating spiritual awakening, psychic opening, consciousness expansion, and the challenges that accompany accelerated growth.

---

> **Important:** This program is an energetic clearing practice. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease, and is not a substitute for professional care.
## Why a Targeted Program?

Spiritual awakening is not always gentle. It can bring confusion, existential crisis, physical symptoms, relationship upheaval, and the dismantling of everything you thought you knew. This program clears the specific blocks, distortions, and interference patterns that arise during awakening — supporting a smoother, more integrated, and more sovereign expansion.
