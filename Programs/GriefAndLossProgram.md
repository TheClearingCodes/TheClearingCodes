# Program: Grief & Loss

A structured multi-session clearing sequence for grief, bereavement, loss, and the deep pain of letting go.

> **Note:** Grief is natural and necessary. This program does not aim to eliminate grief — it clears the *distortions* around grief: the stuck energy, the complicated grief patterns, the inability to process, and the suffering that goes beyond healthy mourning. Honor your grief as you clear.

---

> **Important:** This program is an energetic clearing practice. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease, and is not a substitute for professional care.
## Why a Targeted Program?

Grief is one of the heaviest energies the human system carries. It lodges in the heart, the lungs, the chest, and the entire energy field. When grief cannot fully process — due to shock, cultural suppression, or sheer magnitude — it becomes stuck, creating layers of emotional weight, physical symptoms, and energetic density. This program gently clears each layer while honoring the love that grief represents.

## The 5-Session Sequence

Allow generous time between sessions — grief clearing may bring waves of emotion. Be gentle with yourself.

---

### Session 1: The Weight of Grief

**Focus:** Begin gently by clearing the heaviest, most surface-level grief — the weight, the ache, the tears that won't come or won't stop.

**Step 1 — Center**
> Take a breath. Bring your awareness to the present moment.

**Step 2 — Set Intention**
> "I now begin to gently clear the heaviest layers of grief I am carrying. I honor my grief as an expression of love, and I release only what is ready to release."

**Step 3 — Retrieve**
> "All of the layers of grief, sorrow, loss, heartache, and mourning that are ready to be released from my field? REVEAL, RECEIVE, RECALL, RETRIEVE." (x3)

**Step 4 — Clear**
> "I now clear: all stuck, frozen, or overwhelming grief. All grief that my system has been unable to process. All heaviness, weight, and density of loss in my heart and chest. All numbness and emotional shutdown related to grief. All inability to cry, or inability to stop crying. All the physical symptoms of grief — exhaustion, chest pain, inability to eat, insomnia. All grief that has overstayed its healthy processing time."
>
> "Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds, Vows & Verdicts, Notions & Nines.
> Patterns & Programs, Breaks & Bytes, Roots & Remnants, Ridges & Rites.
> Release, restore, reclaim, refine; all levels and layers, all space and all time."

**Step 5 — Contracts**
> "I rescind all agreements to carry grief beyond its natural purpose. I dissolve all contracts that say I must suffer endlessly to prove my love."
>
> "RESCIND, REVOKE, RECANT, RENOUNCE."

**Step 6 — Install**
> "I now download and activate: gentle healing, comfort, peace, the ability to grieve and also breathe, the capacity to hold both love and loss. My heart is supported. RECEIVE, INSTALL, INTEGRATE."

**Step 7 — Seal**
> "REPAIR, RESET, ANCHOR, LOCK AND SEAL."

## After Completing the Program

- Grief is non-linear — waves may still come. When they do, run a focused clearing
- The Freedom Command (forgiveness) can be powerful for grief work
- Consider journaling as part of your integration
- Be patient with yourself — healing from loss takes time
- Seek support from others — grief shared is grief lightened
