# Program: Health & Physical Body

A structured multi-session clearing sequence for health issues, chronic conditions, pain, and physical body blocks.

> **Important:** This program is an energetic clearing practice. It is not a substitute for professional medical care. Always consult qualified healthcare providers for physical health concerns.

---

## Why a Targeted Program?

Physical health issues often have energetic, emotional, and subconscious components that conventional treatment alone may not address. This program works alongside medical care — clearing the energetic patterns, emotional roots, stored trauma, and inherited predispositions that may be contributing to physical symptoms.
