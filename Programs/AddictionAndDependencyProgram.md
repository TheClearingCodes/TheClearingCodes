# Program: Addiction & Dependency

A structured multi-session clearing sequence for addiction patterns, substance dependencies, behavioral addictions, and compulsive behaviors.

> **Important:** This program is an energetic clearing practice. It is not a substitute for professional addiction treatment, medical detox, or counseling. If you are in active addiction or withdrawal, please seek professional support. This program works alongside — not in place of — professional care.

---

## Why a Targeted Program?

Addiction operates across every level of being — neurological, emotional, energetic, spiritual, ancestral, and identity-based. It involves chemical changes, but also deep energetic hooks, subconscious survival mechanisms, ancestral patterns, and soul-level agreements. This program addresses the energetic dimensions of addiction that conventional treatment may not reach.

## The 5-Session Sequence

---

### Session 1: Breaking the Cycle — Surface Patterns

**Focus:** Clear the most active addiction loops, cravings, and compulsive patterns.

**Step 1 — Center**
> Take a breath. Bring your awareness to the present moment.

**Step 2 — Set Intention**
> "I now clear the active addiction patterns, cravings, and compulsive loops related to [name the addiction/behavior or say 'all addictions and dependencies in my field']."

**Step 3 — Retrieve**
> "All of the cravings, triggers, compulsive patterns, addiction loops, and reward-seeking behaviors related to this addiction? REVEAL, RECEIVE, RECALL, RETRIEVE." (x3)

**Step 4 — Clear**
> "I now clear: all active cravings and urges. All compulsive loops and automatic reaching behaviors. All trigger patterns — situations, emotions, people, and environments that activate the addiction. All reward pathway distortions keeping the cycle running. All addiction webbings and resonance points. All energetic hooks between me and [the substance/behavior]. All romanticization and nostalgia for the addiction."
>
> "Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds, Vows & Verdicts, Notions & Nines.
> Patterns & Programs, Breaks & Bytes, Roots & Remnants, Ridges & Rites.
> Release, restore, reclaim, refine; all levels and layers, all space and all time."

**Step 5 — Contracts**
> "I rescind all agreements with [this substance/behavior]. I dissolve all contracts to return to this addiction. I revoke all energetic bonds with the addictive cycle."
>
> "RESCIND, REVOKE, RECANT, RENOUNCE."

**Step 6 — Install**
> "I now download and activate: freedom from craving, self-regulation, healthy reward pathways, the strength to choose differently, and the peace that my addiction was trying to provide. RECEIVE, INSTALL, INTEGRATE."

**Step 7 — Seal**
> "REPAIR, RESET, ANCHOR, LOCK AND SEAL."

## After Completing the Program

- Continue with professional support — this program complements but does not replace treatment
- Run daily maintenance clearings to support ongoing recovery
- Clear cravings and triggers as they arise using focused clearings
- Build healthy routines, connections, and sources of fulfillment
- The program can be repeated after 2-4 weeks
