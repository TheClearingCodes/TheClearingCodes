# How to Use These Charts

## Overview

This chart system helps you identify specific items that need clearing by progressively narrowing down from broad categories to specific items:

1. **Master Chart** → Identify the major category
2. **Category Chart** → Identify the subcategory
3. **Detail Chart** → Identify the specific item(s)

## The Process

### Step 1: Establish Connection
> "I ask to be shown what most needs clearing for [myself/name] at this time, for my/their highest good."

### Step 2: Test the Master Chart
Ask: "Is there something that needs to be cleared?" If yes, identify which **column** (1-6), then which **row** (A-D).

### Step 3: Go to the Category Chart
Find the corresponding category and identify the **subcategory**.

### Step 4: Identify Specific Items
Within the subcategory, identify the **specific item(s)** that need clearing.

### Step 5: Clear
> "I clear [specific item]. Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds, Vows & Verdicts, Notions & Nines. Release, restore, reclaim, refine."

### Step 6: Verify & Repeat
Test: "Is this fully cleared?" Then: "Is there anything else that needs clearing?"

---

## Testing Methods

**Muscle Testing:**
- Sway Test: Body sways forward = yes, backward = no
- Finger Ring: Strong O = yes, weak = no
- Arm Test: Strong = yes, weak = no

**Pendulum:** Establish your yes/no signals, then point to chart sections.
