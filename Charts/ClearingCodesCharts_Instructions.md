## Muscle Testing & Dowsing Chart System

### How to Use These Charts

## Overview

This chart system helps you identify specific items that need clearing by progressively narrowing down from broad categories to specific items. It follows a simple hierarchy:

1. **Master Chart** → Identify the major category
2. **Category Chart** → Identify the subcategory
3. **Detail Chart** → Identify the specific item(s)

---

## The Process

### Step 1: Center
Before testing, center yourself and set your intention:
> "I ask to be shown what most needs clearing for [myself/name] at this time, for my/their highest good."

### Step 2: Test the Master Chart
Using muscle testing or a pendulum, ask:
> "Is there something that needs to be cleared?"

If yes, identify which **column** (1-6) contains the item, then which **row** (A-D).

### Step 3: Go to the Category Chart
Once you've identified the category, open that category's chart and repeat the process to find the **subcategory**.

### Step 4: Go to the Detail Chart
Within the subcategory, identify the **specific item(s)** that need clearing.

### Step 5: Clear the Item
Once identified, state the clearing command with the specific item:
> "I clear [specific item]. Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds, Vows & Verdicts, Notions & Nines. Release, restore, reclaim, refine."

### Step 6: Verify Completion
Test again to confirm the item has cleared. Ask:
> "Is this item fully cleared?"

If no, run the clearing again or check for related items.

### Step 7: Check for More
Ask:
> "Is there anything else that needs to be cleared at this time?"

Repeat the process until complete.

## Testing Methods

### Muscle Testing (Self-Testing)
- **Sway Test:** Stand relaxed; body sways forward = yes, backward = no
- **Finger Ring:** Make an O with thumb and finger; try to break it - strong = yes, weak = no
- **Arm Test:** Extend arm; have someone press down - strong = yes, weak = no

### Pendulum/Dowsing
- Establish your yes/no signals before beginning
- Hold pendulum over chart sections to identify the correct area
- Can also point to sections and ask "Is it here?"

---

## Tips for Accurate Testing

1. **Stay neutral** - Don't anticipate or hope for specific answers
2. **Stay hydrated** - Dehydration affects accuracy
3. **Ground yourself** - Establish connection before testing
4. **Be specific** - Clear questions get clear answers
5. **Take breaks** - If testing becomes unclear, pause and reset
6. **Trust the process** - Your first response is usually correct

## After Clearing

Once items are cleared, you may optionally:

1. **Install positive frequencies** - Ask what activation would be beneficial
2. **Seal the field** - State: "I seal and protect my field with Source light"
3. **Ground and integrate** - Take a few deep breaths

## Important Notes

- Multiple items may need clearing in one session
- Items may be connected - clearing one may reveal others
- Some items clear instantly; others may need multiple passes
- Trust your testing - even if results seem unexpected
- The charts are guides - intuition may lead you to items not listed
