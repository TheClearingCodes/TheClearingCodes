## How Does It Work?

### The Clearing Command

At the heart of the modality is The Clearing Command:

> "Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds, Vows & Verdicts, Notions & Nines.  
> Patterns & Programs, Breaks & Bytes, Roots & Remnants, Ridges & Rites.  
> Release, restore, reclaim, refine; all levels and layers, all space and all time."

Each word in this command addresses a specific category of interference:

| **Blocks** | Obstacles and obstructions |
| **Binds** | Entanglements and attachments |
| **Loops** | Repeating cycles and patterns |

| **Remnants** | Residual traces after clearing |
| **Ridges** | Accumulated density and energetic walls |
| **Rites** | Ceremonial and ritual bindings |

For detailed explanations of each word, see [TheClearingCommand_DetailedReference.md](TheClearingCommand_DetailedReference.md).

### The Action Phrase

The final line of the command — **"Release, restore, reclaim, refine"** — is the action phrase. It contains four distinct operational instructions that form a complete action sequence:

| Action Word | What It Does |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Release** | Dissolves, transmutes, and returns all cleared items to neutral Source energy. Nothing released can return, reform, or regenerate. |
| **Restore** | Repairs all damage, heals all fractures, and rebalances all systems affected by the cleared items. Fills all cleared spaces with Source light. |
| **Reclaim** | Retrieves all codes, energies, essences, gifts, and aspects of self that have been misdirected, usurped, stolen, suppressed, or taken. |
| **Refine** | Purifies, elevates, and optimizes everything that remains — calibrating the entire being to its highest frequency. |

The action phrase alone — "Release, restore, reclaim, refine" — activates the full process when stated with the intention of running The Clearing Codes.

For full action word definitions, see [ClearingAndActivationProtocol.md](ClearingAndActivationProtocol.md#action-word-definitions).

## Core Principles

### 1. Sovereignty
You are the sole authority over your own field. The clearing process honors and upholds your free will absolutely.

### 2. Source Alignment
All clearings operate through Source-aligned pathways only. No distortion, mimicry, or interference can enter the process.

### 3. Neutrality
The Clearing Codes are not based in duality. There is no "good vs. bad" or "right vs. wrong." They dissolve distortion from a neutral space — without judgment, polarity, or reinforcing the very structures being cleared.

### 4. Comprehensiveness
The command addresses all layers, levels, dimensions, and timelines simultaneously. Nothing is left behind.

### 5. Permanence
What is cleared is permanently cleared — fully dissolved and unable to return, reform, or regenerate. However, similar themes may have multiple layers (childhood, ancestral, karmic, collective), and clearing one layer may reveal the next. Additionally, daily life continuously introduces new energetic material. This is why regular clearing is recommended — not because previous clearings failed, but because you are always going deeper and your field is always encountering the world.

### 6. Replacement
Cleared spaces are immediately filled with Source light and positive frequencies.

### 7. Protection
The process includes built-in protections that operate automatically throughout.

### 8. Simplicity
Despite its comprehensive nature, the modality is simple to use. Even the shortest command version activates the full process.

Healing tools should not be gatekept. If something can help people clear suffering and limitation from their lives, it should be available to everyone — without barriers, conditions, or someone deciding who is worthy enough to access it.

The Clearing Codes are not just free — they are open source. Everything is yours to use: for yourself, your clients, your courses, your guided meditations, your workshops, your retreats, your recordings, your own modality. You are always free to create your own original works — courses, programs, guided meditations, session scripts, and even entire modalities — that use and reference The Clearing Codes. The Clearing Codes exist because healing should be a right, not a privilege.
