## The Process

**1. Center** (Optional)  
Take a breath. Bring your awareness to the present moment.  
Open with: *"ALIGN."*

**2. Focus**  
Bring to mind what you want to clear - a specific issue, pattern, feeling, or simply "everything not aligned with my highest good."

**3. State the Command**  
Choose any version below - all are equally effective.

**4. Receive**  
Allow the clearing to integrate. Close with:  
"Anchor, lock and seal. Complete."  
*"SEAL and SHIELD."*

Repeat as needed.
