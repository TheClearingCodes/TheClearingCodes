## The Clearing Command

> "Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds, Vows & Verdicts, Notions & Nines.
> Patterns & Programs, Breaks & Bytes, Roots & Remnants, Ridges & Rites.
> Release, restore, reclaim, refine; all levels and layers, all space and all time."

1. Think of what you want to clear
2. State the Clearing Command (aloud or silently)
3. Allow the clearing to integrate

That's it. The command contains 16 clearing words addressing every category of interference, and 4 action words that form a complete clearing sequence. An automated protocol operates in the background — handling protection, transmutation, filling of cleared spaces, and sealing — without requiring you to manage any of it consciously.

## Why Open Source?

Healing tools should not be gatekept behind paywalls or certifications. The Clearing Codes are open source because if something can help people clear suffering from their lives, it should be available to everyone. Everything here — the commands, the process, the guides, the clearing lists — is yours to use, teach, record, and build upon. There are no hidden levels to unlock and no membership required. The documents must remain unmodified when shared, so the system stays intact and works as designed — but you are free to create your own original works that use and reference The Clearing Codes. Healing should be a right, not a privilege.
