## Session Tips

Before beginning, set yourself up for the best possible session:

- **Hydrate** — Drink water before and after. Dehydration affects energy flow and your body's ability to process.
- **Open body language** — Uncross your arms and legs. This opens your bioenergetic field to a state of receiving.
- **Sit comfortably** — A relaxed body allows the mind and energy system to open.
- **Breathe** — Take a few deep breaths before starting. This activates the parasympathetic nervous system and moves you into a receptive state.
- **Minimize distractions** — Phone on silent. Give yourself the space to be fully present.
- **During the session** — Maintain open posture, breathe naturally, and trust whatever comes up (tears, yawning, tingling, heat, cold, or nothing at all are all normal).

These simple adjustments improve session quality, clearing depth, and muscle testing accuracy significantly.

## Step 1: The Center Command
*Preparation & Alignment — State once at the beginning of a session*

**Purpose:** Center yourself and establish the clearing field.

**Open with:**
> *"I now CENTER and ALIGN."*

*ALIGN (Activate, Link, Insulate, Ground & Neutralize) is the opening protocol — state it once at the beginning of any session.*

**State:**
> "Through these clearings and codes, I command and declare that everything not aligned to my highest good and my Original Life-Affirming blueprint is permanently located, collapsed, cleared, dissolved, transformed, terminated and transmuted, in the highest and best way, for my highest good and for the good of all."

## Step 2: The Retrieval Command
*Surface blocks and limitations for clearing*

**Purpose:** Bring to awareness everything that needs to be cleared.

**State:**
> "All of the decisions, beliefs, agreements, attachments, distortions, and interference — all UNIVERSALs, known and unknown — across all layers, levels, and timelines."

**State 3 times:**
> "REVEAL, RECEIVE, RECALL, RETRIEVE."

## Step 5: The Final Sweep
*Catch any hidden, covert, or concealed items*

**Purpose:** Ensure nothing escapes clearing by addressing hidden elements.

**State:**
> "Everything CLOAKED and VEILED — all UNUSUALs — everything missed, skipped, and left behind — and everything anchoring it in place — is cleared, dissolved, and transmuted now."

**Then repeat the Clearing Command (any version):**
> "Loops & Lines, Blocks & Binds, Vows & Verdicts, Notions & Nines.  
> Release, restore, reclaim, refine. All levels and layers, all space and all time."
