## Important: All Versions Are Equally Powerful

Any version of the Clearing Command activates the complete clearing process with full power and comprehensiveness. The shorthand versions are energetically equivalent to stating the full command. Intent and invocation trigger the complete protocol; brevity does not diminish effectiveness.

- All 16 clearing words are activated regardless of which version is spoken
- All layers, levels, space, and time are addressed regardless of version used
- The command works completely whether spoken aloud, whispered, or stated silently/internally

## The 16 Clearing Words

The Clearing Command contains 16 specific clearing words organized in 8 pairs. Each word addresses a distinct category of interference.

| Word | Pair | Primary Function |
|------|------|------------------|
| **Blocks** | Blocks & Binds | Removes obstacles and obstructions |
| **Binds** | Blocks & Binds | Releases entanglements and energetic ties |
| **Loops** | Loops & Lines | Breaks repetitive cycles |
| **Lines** | Loops & Lines | Dissolves boundaries, divisions, and ancestral lines |
| **Vows** | Vows & Verdicts | Clears commitments, pledges, and oaths |
| **Verdicts** | Vows & Verdicts | Releases judgments and sentences |
| **Notions** | Notions & Nines | Dissolves mental constructs and beliefs |
| **Nines** | Notions & Nines | Collapses illusion, identity traps, and the "game" |
| **Patterns** | Patterns & Programs | Clears ingrained behavioral blueprints |
| **Programs** | Patterns & Programs | Removes installed conditioning systems |
| **Breaks** | Breaks & Bytes | Heals fractures and disconnections |
| **Bytes** | Breaks & Bytes | Clears digital and AI-based influences |
| **Roots** | Roots & Remnants | Addresses foundational origins of issues |
| **Remnants** | Roots & Remnants | Clears residual traces after clearing |
| **Ridges** | Ridges & Rites | Dissolves accumulated density and energetic walls |
| **Rites** | Ridges & Rites | Releases ceremonial and ritual bindings |

**Together, these 16 words create a comprehensive clearing that addresses every level of existence — mental, emotional, physical, energetic, karmic, ancestral, collective, and multidimensional — across all time, space, dimensions, and realities.**

## The 4 Action Words

The action phrase — "Release, restore, reclaim, refine" — contains four distinct operational instructions that form a complete action sequence:

| Action Word | Function | What It Triggers |
|-------------|----------|-----------------|
| **Release** | Let go, dissolve, transmute | Full dissolution and return to neutral Source energy |
| **Restore** | Repair, heal, rebalance | Repair of all damage, rebalancing of all systems |
| **Reclaim** | Take back, retrieve, reassert sovereignty | Retrieval of all stolen/misdirected codes, energies, and aspects of self |
| **Refine** | Purify, elevate, optimize | Purification and elevation beyond previous baseline |
