# The Clearing Codes — Glossary of Terms

This glossary provides plain-language definitions for all specialized terms used throughout The Clearing Codes documentation. Terms are listed alphabetically. For thematic groupings, see the [Topic Index](#topic-index) at the end.

> **Note:** Many of these terms originate from various spiritual, energetic, and consciousness traditions. The definitions here reflect how they are used *within this modality* and may not match definitions found elsewhere.

## A

**Acceleration Codes**
Energetic codes that speed up the process of integrating activations and upgrades. Part of the sacred code activation system.

**Activations**
The process of installing or turning on positive frequencies, qualities, and energetic states after clearing. The counterpart to clearing — clearing removes what doesn't serve; activations install what does.

**Action Phrase**
The four action words of the Clearing Command: "Release, restore, reclaim, refine." Each word triggers a specific set of processes. See [ClearingAndActivationProtocol.md](ClearingAndActivationProtocol.md#action-word-definitions) for full definitions.

**ALIGN**
A core protocol for opening and preparing a clearing session. Stands for: Activate, Link, Insulate, Ground & Neutralize. Activates field protection, forms the energetic column (Source to Earth) connecting only with Source-consciousness and Life-affirming healers, guardians, and guides, establishes containment so cleared energies remain in their own fields, grounds into the Earth, and neutralizes interference in the working space. Run at the start of every session. See also: *SEAL*, *SHIELD*.

**Akashic Records**
A metaphysical concept of a universal energetic "library" that stores all information about every soul's experiences across all lifetimes. Within this modality, manipulation of Akashic Records is something that can be cleared.

**Anchor, Lock, Heal, Protect & Seal**
The sealing declaration used to complete and lock in clearing and activation work. Ensures all changes are permanent, protected, and self-maintaining. Optional to state aloud — the Protocol runs this automatically.

**Applied Kinesiology**
A diagnostic technique that tests muscle strength to identify energetic imbalances. Also called muscle testing. Can be used to identify what needs clearing or to confirm that clearing has occurred. See also: *Muscle Testing*, *Dowsing*.

**Astral Body**
One of the subtle energy bodies in the multidimensional system. Associated with emotions, dreams, and out-of-body experiences. The astral field is where dream and sleep-state experiences occur.

**Astral Debris**
Energetic residue, fragments, or waste material accumulated in the astral field — often from unprocessed emotions, dreams, or external interference.

**Attractor Field / Attractor Point**
An energetic pattern or structure that draws certain experiences, people, or circumstances toward you — either beneficial or life-diminishing. Clearing removes life-diminishing attractor fields.

**Aura / Auric Field**
The multi-layered energy field surrounding the physical body. Composed of multiple layers (etheric, emotional, mental, astral, causal, celestial, ketheric, and others). Can develop holes, fractures, tears, or distortions that are healed during clearing.

**Timeline**

**Torsion Field**
A field generated by spinning or rotating objects/energies. In the energetic context, torsion fields carry information and can influence matter and consciousness at a distance.

**Transformation Statement**
A supplementary verbal command: "CLEAR, DISSOLVE, TRANSFORM, TRANSMUTE." Provides additional clearing intensity.

The process of converting lower-frequency, distorted, or life-diminishing energy into neutral Source energy. More thorough than simply "releasing" — transmutation ensures nothing harmful remains or can re-form.

**Trauma Bonds**
Emotional attachments that form through shared traumatic experiences. These energetic ties can keep a person connected to harmful people or situations. Cleared as part of relationship and emotional clearing.

**Tri-wave**
A trinity-based energetic current — a three-part wave structure that is self-sustaining, non-polarized, and Source-aligned. Unlike bi-wave systems that require opposition to function, tri-wave currents are inherently balanced. This is the energetic foundation the modality operates through. See also: *Bi-wave*.

**12-Strand DNA**
The concept that the full human DNA template contains 12 strands (rather than the 2 currently active in physical biology), with the additional strands existing as energetic templates. Activation of higher strand counts is part of the DNA activation system.

**12-Tree Grid**
An energetic grid structure representing the organic tree of life — connected to the 12 dimensions and the Life-Affirming creation template.
